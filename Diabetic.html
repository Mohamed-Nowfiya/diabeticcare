What Cause Diabetetes?

The condition where pancreas is no longer able to make insulin, or when the body cannot make good use of the insulin it produces.

Type 1 diabetes
  • - children and adolescents with very little or no insulin

    • Type 2 diabetes
  • - common in adults , not make good use of the insulin body produce

    • Gestational diabetes (GDM)
  • - high blood glucose during pregnancy

    • Symptoms:
    • -increased thirst
    • -increased hunger
    • -numbness in the feet
    • -unexpected Weight loss

    Prevention

    Diabetes is chronic disease but we can control it in limit by following Healthy Foods

    Blood Glucose

    The blood glucose level is the amount of glucose in the blood. comes from the foods we eat

    List of Healthy Foods To main Glucose Level

  • Raw, Cooked, or Roasted Vegetables

  • Greens

  • Flavorful, Low-calorie Drinks

  • Melon or Berries

  • Whole-grain, Higher-fiber Foods

  • Protein

    • Normal Glucose Range
    List of Healthy Foods to lower Glucose Level

  • Broccoli and broccoli sprouts

  • Pumpkin and pumpkin seeds

  • Nuts and nut butter

  • Oats and oat bran

  • Berries,Avocados,Citrus fruits,Eggs and Apples

  • Okra,Beans and lentils

    • High Glucose Range
    List of Healthy Foods to increase Glucose Level

  • peanut butter and crackers

  • 100% grape juice

  • medjool dates, raisins and applesauce

  • bananas,grapes and pineapple

  • maple syrup

  • Carbohydrate Rich Food

    • Low Glucose Range
